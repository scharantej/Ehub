E-commerce Basics

What is e-commerce?

E-commerce, or electronic commerce, is the buying and selling of goods or services over the internet.

Types of e-commerce businesses

  • Business-to-business (B2B)
  • Business-to-consumer (B2C)
  • Consumer-to-consumer (C2C)

Building an e-commerce website

There are many different ways to build an e-commerce website. You can use a pre-built platform like Shopify or WooCommerce, or you can hire a web developer to create a custom website for you.

Marketing strategies for e-commerce businesses

  • Search engine optimization (SEO)
  • Social media marketing
  • Email marketing

Payment gateways for e-commerce businesses

  • PayPal
  • Stripe
  • Square
